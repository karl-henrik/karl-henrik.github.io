### Bio

I am Karl-Henrik Nilsson, an experienced developer, Microsoft MVP and Internet of Things aficionado. I live in Sweden, a place that is as lovely at summer as dark and dreary during winter but at least I don't have to worry about screen glare! I am fortunate enough to have a bit of a speaking career that allows me to see some truly fantastic places and meet some truly amazing people around the world!

### Consultant

I took the leap from working with radio/cellphone station development in 2010 and joined Sogeti. Since then, I have worked as a Microcontroller/C++/C# .NET technology consultant. Working as a consultant allows me to divide my time between all the things I love to do, such as learning new things, sharing knowledge and working with lots of different solutions and technology.

Since 2015 I am a [Ninja](https://www.tretton37.com/who-we-are) at [tretton37](http://tretton37.com) splitting my time between building a leet ninja team in [Dalarna](https://www.google.se/maps/place/Dalarna+County/@61.0628921,13.3175066,8z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x4667d90f946ad8a7:0x3034506de8c8a90!8m2!3d61.0917012!4d14.6663653) and developing fantastic applications and hardware solutions for our customers.
