### Sessions

I love interacting with the community, and my favourite way of all is definitively as a speaker. Perhaps my love for public speaking is why I, since 2013, have been a high rated, recurring speaker on stages around Europe. 

I firmly believe that delivering good content is the most important thing for a speaker. But great content is worthless if you can't communicate it well to your audience and keep their engagement. This is why I have developed a presentation style that mix great content with entertaining and engaging stories.    

### Abstracts

These are the sessions I am currently blabbering about.

#### Title: *“IoT - The Internet of (less) useless things"*
The internet of things is growing quickly and people are producing products by the hour, but how useful is a tweeting refrigerator or a ring that sends condolences to your family when your hearth stops beating? IoT is much bigger than hardware and building good products require all sorts of software development and skills. Let's talk about designing and building things that interact with the world in a way that might actually benefit you! 


#### Title: *“Hacking your home – reverse engineering wireless transmissions”* 
The internet of things is built around sensors, without sensors we don’t know what goes on and we can’t tell our software to make rational choices based on the analytics of this data, but building our own sensors are not always practical or cost efficient when there are numerous of the shelf solution that could do the same thing if we could only access their data! This talk demonstrates how to reverse engineer standard sensor wireless protocols from weather stations, movement sensors and more to use for your own designs!”

#### Title: Oh a blog post!
Learning today can be tough! You spend your days reading blog posts, listening to talks and podcasts but are you really learning or are you mearly wasting time? A few years back I noticed that I spent an increasing amount of time "learning" but without any real "gains". This talk discusses how I improved my own learning, different ways that I use for studying and how I found out how I learn and make the most of my already scarce free time.
